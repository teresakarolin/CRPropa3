Detailed Description

Rectangular box with periodic boundaries.

If a particle passes on of the sides it is placed at the opposite side and its initial (source) position changed accordingly. This realizes periodic boundaries, where the particle is kept inside the box and the source is moved away from the box. Particles can overshoot (be outside of the box during the step) since the step size is not limited by this module.
